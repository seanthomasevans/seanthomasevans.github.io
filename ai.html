Conformer

A production pipeline that compiles a screenplay into coverage shotlists and routes them to image-to-video models. A custom Fountain and Final Draft parser turns a script into scene, beat, and shot structure; coverage formulas are implemented as code; prompts compile across Flux, Hunyuan, Kling, World Labs, Veo, and Wan with camera-motion mapping over seventeen motions and five models. Budget-gated dispatch, face-identity validation on every take, a JSONL provenance ledger, and assembly straight into DaVinci Resolve. Hardened on a feature at 774 shots and 2,558 takes; deployed on RTX PRO 6000 hardware.

TRIYO AML platform

An anti-money-laundering platform built with Triyo for its Libro Credit Union engagement: eight compliance agents for transaction monitoring, sanctions and PEP screening, due diligence, and network analysis, on Azure with FINTRAC Gen 2 schemas parsed across 884 properties and a hash-chained audit ledger. The standout piece is a crowd-simulation-inspired synthetic-data engine, where ordinary members carry legitimate spending rules and criminals carry a few more, so fifteen laundering typologies emerge naturally inside a 1.2-million-transaction stream with automatic ground-truth labels. That labeled data scores fifteen behavioral detectors at 0.958 precision.

Evaluation harnesses

Objective checks across the pipelines, on the principle that the system producing the work is never its own judge. A multi-model judge scores copy, code, art, and audio across two frontier models in parallel; ground-truth harnesses catch monotone voice clones by pitch variance and broken video by optical flow; the AML benchmark gates detector precision and recall against fixed thresholds in continuous integration.
