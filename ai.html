01Conformer · screenplay to validated takes

Screenplay → shotlist → takes

Conformer

Architect and developer. A production CLI and library that compiles screenplay material into coverage shotlists and routes them to image-to-video models. A custom Fountain and Final Draft parser turns a script into scene, beat, and shot DNA. Standard coverage formulas, wide, mediums, an over-the-shoulder pair, and the emotional close-up, are implemented as code.

Prompt compilation across Flux, Hunyuan, Kling, World Labs 3D, Veo, and Wan. Camera-motion mapping over seventeen motions and five models. Budget-gated dispatch. Four-tier take classification with face-identity validation on every take. A JSONL provenance ledger. FCPXML and EDL assembly into DaVinci Resolve. Hardened on Bruce McDonald’s War of 1812.
