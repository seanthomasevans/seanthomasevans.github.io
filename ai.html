Agentic work · 2023 to present

Two and a half years of agents

Late 2023 · the first build

WordPress corpus to conversational RAG

The first agent-solved-and-validated build: extraction of a fifteen-year WordPress site into a functional RAG system with an ElevenLabs voice and a conversational interface. Agents handled the extraction and structuring; validation confirmed the corpus survived the migration intact.

Harness pattern

Harnesses instead of one-offs

Recurring production problems get a harness: a PRD for what the system needs, an agent survey of the method space, unattended batch execution, and an eval loop trained on keep and kill decisions. The Lanois Time Machine is the canonical example: a hundred clips in, multiple slowdown profiles out, review in two hours instead of days.

Validation

Objective checks, human gates

Every generated take passes face-identity validation and a four-tier classification before review. Ground-truth harnesses catch monotone voice clones by pitch variance and broken video by optical flow. Approval stays human, recorded on an audit trail; the producing system is never the judge of its own output.

Provenance

Defensible by construction

JSONL provenance ledgers on every generation, a five-level likeness-and-synthesis ladder with per-model indemnity mapping for VFX work, and hash-chained audit logs for regulated casework. Built for deliverables that get audited: a Warner release, an Amazon series, compliance casework.

One move, five industries

Applied across industries

Music · Daniel Lanois, Warner Records era

The Maker, an archive engine over Lanois’s personal collection: thousands of files indexed, 95 sessions transcribed into 18,992 segments, 221,000 chord events mapped with beat-synced harmony analysis, stems separated, thematic cuts assembled directly into DaVinci Resolve. The same project carries the typewriter-key title system, gear mapping and audio-plugin development cloning the PT93, a freeze performance setup for guitar and pedal steel, a NAM, RAVE, and impulse-capture pipeline, voice cloning, and a dialogue-to-animation pipeline. On top of it, Belladonna Nocturne: four visual companions shipped for the June 2026 Warner release, two of them the album’s lead singles.

Television · Amazon Prime

The No Dress Rehearsal pipeline turned thousands of archival stills into 4K motion for a TIFF-premiered Prime series: chained models for face restoration, depth synthesis, and temporal interpolation, run at series scale.

Exhibition · Design Exchange, Toronto

Athena: chat-driven generation of image, video, and 3D at the exact pixel dimensions of roughly thirty-one named projection surfaces, DXV encoding, Resolume REST playback control, a PlayCanvas venue twin, and anamorphic perspective rendering. Built, demoed on the trading floor, delivered as a Phase 1 alpha. Documented unit economics: a full event content package for a fraction of traditional production cost.

Research · AI Alpha

An agentic equity-research engine: filings, transcripts, news, and macro data extracted into per-issuer and per-sector signals, feeding a mean-CVaR portfolio optimizer with human PM sign-off. The pitch decks and an interactive PM console are live.

Regulated finance · TRIYO

An AML platform built with Triyo, the RegTech vendor, for its Libro Credit Union engagement. The standout piece is a crowd-simulation-inspired synthetic-data engine: agent-based actors carry legitimate spending rules, criminal actors carry a few extra, and fifteen laundering typologies emerge naturally inside a 1.2-million-transaction stream with automatic ground-truth labels. That labeled stream trains and scores fifteen behavioral detectors, measured at 0.958 precision on the scored set. Around it: eight compliance agents, FINTRAC Gen 2 schemas parsed across 884 properties and 256 required fields, a hash-chained WORM audit ledger, and a deployed, password-gated AI-ops console on Azure.

Evaluation harnesses

The system never judges its own work

A multi-model judge scores copy, code, art, and audio across two frontier models in parallel. Ground-truth harnesses catch monotone voice clones by pitch variance against the real voice, and frozen or melted generated video by optical flow. The AML benchmark gates detector precision and recall against fixed thresholds in CI. Written methodology underneath all of it: falsifiable success first, objective validation, human gate last.

Local video pipeline

Fully local, HDR-finished

A self-contained video-generation pipeline pairing high-quality keyframes with open-weight motion models on a 96GB GPU, finishing to true HDR ProRes masters through a 32-bit EXR chain. Every clip is graded by an automated evaluation harness before a human pass, and the pipeline benchmarks its own fidelity gap against closed providers. Deploys the same stack to cloud GPUs on demand.
