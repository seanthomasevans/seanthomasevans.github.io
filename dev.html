Development

Production pipelines and systems since 2003, from database-driven shot management for broadcast animation to stereo and 360° immersive pipelines and, most recently, agentic AI. The recurring job is the same: build the machinery that lets a small team move a large volume of work through review and out the door.

Foundations
The tools and techniques underneath the projects above, grouped by what they do.

Generative & ML

Diffusion (Flux, Stable Diffusion), image-to-video (Kling, Veo, Sora, Wan, Hunyuan, LTX), 3D generation (Hunyuan3D, Trellis, World Labs), audio (Whisper, Demucs, custom harmonic analysers), voice (ElevenLabs, cloning), persona pipelines (FaceFusion, LoRA training), style transfer, NeRF and Gaussian splatting, world models, photogrammetric twins, volumetric capture.

Agentic systems

From AutoGPT and LangChain to Claude with Model Context Protocol servers and skills: multi-agent orchestration with explicit role and tool boundaries, agent-based synthetic-data simulation, provenance ledgers, indemnity-aware model routing, evaluation harnesses with ground-truth gating.

Pipelines & infra

Database-driven production management (PHP/MySQL through to modern stacks), stereo and 360° capture and delivery, TouchDesigner, Resolume (REST control), GLSL, PLY point clouds, MIDI, Kooima CAVE projection, projection mapping at venue scale.

Compute & render farms

High-performance compute, storage, and network design, cooling, and systems management and monitoring. Large render farms with distributed-computing workflows and render management, and remote and cloud rendering (AWS from its early days).

Stack

Python, FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Pydantic, Hono, Vite, React, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, Redis, Azure with hash-chained audit logging for regulated workloads.

Art & craft
The hands-on craft alongside the systems, from post-production finishing to sound, electronics, and physical fabrication.

Direction & post

Animation direction, editorial and colour (Premiere, DaVinci Resolve), compositing and matte painting, and photoreal CGI.

Design & type

Typography and title design, projection design, and fine-art print production. Digital art support with Nigel Scott: Photoshop and Procreate, scanning and media capture, and finishing and pipeline workflow.

Sound design

Sound design and audio craft, stem separation, harmonic analysis, and mix finishing.

Music

Guitar and pedal-steel performance rigs, freeze and sustain setups, and audio-plugin development, including a PT93 clone.

Electronics

Electronics and hardware design, audio studio equipment and performance interfaces, and digital model capture and implementation: RAVE, NAM, and impulse-response capture and management.

Fabrication

3D printing and physical fabrication. Paper-sculpture fabrication for artist Dean Drever, the eagle and the bears: a large edition in card stock and a smaller, tighter-tolerance one on regular paper.
