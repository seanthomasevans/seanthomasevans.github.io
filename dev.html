Development

Production pipelines and systems since 2003, from database-driven shot management for broadcast animation to stereo and 360° immersive pipelines and, most recently, agentic AI: machinery that lets a small team move a large volume of work through review and out the door.

Foundations
The tools and techniques underneath the projects above, grouped by what they do.

Diffusion (Flux, Stable Diffusion), image-to-video (Kling, Veo, Sora, Wan, Hunyuan, LTX), audio and voice (Whisper, Demucs, ElevenLabs, voice cloning, custom harmonic analysers), persona pipelines (FaceFusion, LoRA training), and style transfer.

3D & capture

3D generation (Hunyuan3D, Trellis, World Labs), NeRF and Gaussian splatting, world models, photogrammetric twins, and volumetric capture.

Database-driven production management (PHP/MySQL through to modern stacks), stereo and 360° capture and delivery, TouchDesigner, Resolume (REST control), GLSL, PLY point clouds, MIDI, Kooima CAVE projection, projection mapping at venue scale.

Compute & render farms

High-performance compute, storage, and network design, cooling, and systems management and monitoring. Large render farms with distributed-computing workflows and render management, and remote and cloud rendering (AWS from its early days).

Stack
