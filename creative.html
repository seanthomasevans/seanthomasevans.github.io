Daniel Lanois · The Maker

The Maker is the harness side of the Lanois work: an archive engine over his personal film and audio collection, with thousands of files indexed, 95 sessions transcribed into 18,992 segments, 221,000 chord events mapped with beat-synced harmony analysis, stems separated, and curated thematic cuts assembled directly into DaVinci Resolve. The typewriter-key title system and the ultra-slow-motion Time Machine harness live here.

It also carries the audio and performance rigs: gear mapping and audio-plugin development cloning the PT93, a freeze performance setup for guitar and pedal steel, a NAM, RAVE, and impulse-capture pipeline, voice cloning, and a dialogue-and-story-to-animation pipeline.

Athena · Design Exchange

A generative-content system built for the Design Exchange’s wrap-around trading floor: chat-driven image, video, and 3D generation at the exact pixel dimensions of the venue’s surfaces, encoded to Resolume’s DXV format with REST playback control, a PlayCanvas venue twin, and anamorphic perspective rendering. Built, demoed on the floor, and delivered as a Phase 1 alpha.

Alannah Myles · restoration and personas

AI restoration of standard-definition archival music-video footage, rebuilt to 4K with noise reduction and a 16:9 reformat while keeping the 1990s look: What Am I Going to Do With You. On House of Love, the work extended to a shot-by-shot face-and-mouth replacement pipeline built in-house with FaceFusion, ComfyUI, Flux, and trained LoRAs for character consistency. Five videos delivered across the engagement.

Also now An animated Tragically Hip spot for Kawartha Dairy. AI generative pipelines for Johnny Fay of The Tragically Hip.
