Creative technologist and producer. I build AI production systems for music, film, and exhibition, after a career directing visual effects and producing immersive work.

It covers a lot of ground, and I have never had a tidy answer for what I do. The short version: I build the systems and pipelines that get difficult projects made, across film, music, exhibition, and lately AI. Rather than try to explain it, this site shows the work, in the two halves it tends to fall into.
